I work in the [Mesbah Lab](https://www.mesbahlab.com) whose research is at the forefront of applying advanced control theory, machine learning, and applied mathematics to the analysis and control of complex chemical and biological systems.

I am currently working on a project that involves the implementation of learning-based approximate model predictive control (MPC) on embedded systems. The desired goal is the development of a *fast* and *safe* control strategy for non-equilibrium plasmas (NEPs), which have uses in medicine, bioprocessing, catalysis, etc.
